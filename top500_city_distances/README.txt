Top 500 city distance matrix
============================

Rows kept: 500
Matrix definition: entry (i,j) is great-circle distance between cities i and j.
Units: distance_matrix_km.csv is kilometers; distance_matrix_miles.csv is miles.
Diagonal entries are 0. Matrix is symmetric.
Source URL: https://gist.githubusercontent.com/dikaio/0ce2a7e9f7088918f8c6ff24436fd035/raw/data.csv
